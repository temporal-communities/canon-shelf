# »Die 100 besten Bücher des 21. Jahrhunderts« (NZZ-Kanon der Gegenwart)

*= »The 100 Best Books of the 21st Century« (NZZ Canon of the Present)*

Teaser: *»Welche Bücher haben uns seit dem Jahr 2000 geprägt? Dies ist unser Kanon der Gegenwart – von der Redaktion der ›NZZ am Sonntag‹ ausgewählt.«*

This canon was published in Swiss-German Sunday newspaper »Neue Zürcher Zeitung am Sonntag« on 24 November 2024 in their quarterly supplement »Bücher am Sonntag«, which can be downloaded [here as PDF](https://files.static-nzz.ch/2024/11/24/31520c6f-dd29-4cca-9697-8a0132dc2f53.pdf). There is a variant in [their online edition](https://www.nzz.ch/feuilleton/die-100-besten-buecher-des-21-jahrhunderts-ld.1857099) in which info on books is somewhat sparser.

We transformed the canon into a machine-readable list and enriched it with metadata on authors and works. Publication years generally refer to the first edition in the original language (we double-checked this info and corrected it where necessary).

The list includes both literary works and non-fiction, as well as book series (*Persepolis* by Marjane Satrapi, *Min kamp* by Karl Ove Knausgård, *Vernon Subutex* by Virginie Despentes, *The Helsinki Trilogy* by Pirkko Saisio). The essay collection *Der Spass an der Sache* contains all of David Foster Wallace's essays and was published in German translation in 2018, combining three different volumes originally published in English in 1997, 2005 and 2012.
