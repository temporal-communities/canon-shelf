# »Die 100 besten Bücher der Welt – 1925 bis 2025: Der internationale SPIEGEL-Kanon«
*(= »The 100 Best Books – 1925 to 2025: The international SPIEGEL Literary Canon«)*

This canon was published in German weekly »Der Spiegel« no. 13 (22 March 2025, pp. 9–33, cf. [their online edition](https://www.spiegel.de/kultur/literatur/literatur-die-besten-buecher-der-welt-zwischen-1925-und-2025-der-spiegel-literaturkanon-a-e138f11e-1302-48d2-8476-56840777d4dd), paywalled). The four-member canon committee included: Eva Horn, Peter Sloterdijk, Miryam Schellbach, Michael Maar.

We transformed the canon into a machine-readable list and enriched it with metadata on authors and works (Author Wikidata ID, Author GND ID, Work Wikidata ID, Work Goodreads ID, Work GND ID).
